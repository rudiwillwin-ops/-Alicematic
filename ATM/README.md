# NeutralGridLite (MT5)

## Important: MT5 Login Details
MetaTrader 5 does **not** allow Expert Advisors (EAs) to log in to trading accounts or to store/submit your account credentials. Login is handled by the MT5 terminal itself for security reasons.

To log in:
1. Open MT5.
2. Go to **File → Login to Trade Account**.
3. Enter your account number, password, and server.

## Auto-Start Behavior
This EA starts trading automatically when attached to a chart because `AutoStart = true` by default. The on-chart Start/Stop button remains available for manual control.

## How to Run
1. Open `NeutralGridLite.mq5` in MetaEditor and compile.
2. In MT5, open a chart and attach the EA.
3. Enable **Algo Trading** in MT5.
